Uninstall Instructions

We're sorry to see you go! Here's how to uninstall EGuideSearches from your browser.

Chrome - Method 1

  1. Open Chrome and click the three dots menu (⋮) in the top-right corner
  2. Select "More tools" → "Extensions"
  3. Find EGuideSearches in the list
  4. Click "Remove"
  5. Confirm deletion when prompted

Chrome - Method 2

  1. Right-click on the EGuideSearches icon in your browser toolbar
  2. Select "Remove from Chrome..."
  3. Confirm deletion

Microsoft Edge

  1. Click the three dots menu in the top-right corner
  2. Select "Extensions"
  3. Find EGuideSearches
  4. Click "Remove"
  5. Confirm when prompted

What Gets Removed

Uninstalling automatically:

  • Removes all extension files
  • Clears stored preferences
  • Removes the toolbar icon
  • No additional cleanup required

Troubleshooting

If you experience issues uninstalling:

  • Restart your browser and try again
  • Ensure you have administrator access
  • Close all browser windows before uninstalling
  • Contact support if problems persist
