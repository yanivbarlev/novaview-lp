Privacy Policy

EGuideSearches ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected:

  • Search queries you enter
  • Browser type and version
  • Usage data and interaction patterns
  • IP address and general location

Voluntarily Provided: Information you provide when contacting support

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Extension's functionality
  • To improve search results and user experience
  • To display relevant advertisements
  • To respond to your inquiries

Advertising

The Extension is ad-supported. Third-party advertising partners may collect browsing activity data. You can opt out through browser settings or the Network Advertising Initiative (optout.networkadvertising.org).

Information Sharing

We share data with:

  • Service providers who help operate the Extension
  • Advertising partners
  • Legal authorities when required by law

Data Security & Retention

We implement security measures to protect your information, though no method is 100% secure. Search data is typically retained for up to 90 days.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Opt out of data collection
  • Uninstall the extension at any time
